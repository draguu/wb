Our Story

A Decade of Pixels,
Purpose, and Persistence

2014
Studio Founded

Pratik Solanki and Niyal Parmar co-founded Weybec Studio in Vadodara, Gujarat — with a vision to build a world-class CGI studio 100% on Blender, before it was cool.

2016
Blender Addons for the Community

We built and distributed Blender plugins to the global CG community, getting cited on Quora among India's top Blender-native studios.

2019
Ad Films Go International

Our 3D ad film pipeline matured. We began delivering broadcast-quality commercials for FMCG, automotive, and pharma clients across India and abroad.

2022
Original IP: Bhurji Wale Chacha

We launched our own Hindi adult animated comedy series on YouTube — original characters, original humor, original ownership.

2024
Working with Globally Known Brands

We delivered animation for globally recognised brands across FMCG, broadcast, and entertainment — Britannia, Zee, SonyYAY, and more — cementing our place on the international stage.

2026
Expanding Globally

Weybec is scaling its presence beyond India — pitching original kids IP to global platforms, growing the team, and taking on international co-productions. The world is next.
